Frequently asked questions

If you haven't found the answer to your question about working as a taxi driver below, please contact us and we will try to help.

What documents do I need to work as a taxi driver?

All platforms (Bolt, Uber, FreeNow) require drivers to have a valid Polish driving license, a certificate of no criminal record, medical and psychological tests, and a taxi ID. We help you obtain all necessary documents.

Are there bonuses and promotions?

All platforms (Bolt, Uber, FreeNow) regularly run promotional campaigns with bonuses. You can earn from multiple platforms simultaneously, maximizing your bonus potential.

How long does driver verification take?

Driver verification typically takes 1 to 2 business days per platform. We handle the registration process for all platforms (Bolt, Uber, FreeNow) simultaneously to get you started faster.

Is there a limit to online working hours?

Most platforms limit online time to 12 hours for safety. After that, you need to take a break before logging in again. This applies to Bolt, Uber, and FreeNow.

When do I get paid?

Payment schedules vary by platform: Bolt and FreeNow pay weekly (Monday for previous week), while Uber includes the first four hours of Monday. Working on all platforms means multiple income streams throughout the week.

Can I work on multiple platforms at the same time?

Yes! This is one of our main advantages. We register you with Bolt, Uber, and FreeNow simultaneously. You can switch between apps or use them together to maximize your earnings and minimize downtime between rides.
